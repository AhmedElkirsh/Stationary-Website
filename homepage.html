About Us

Welcome to pencil perfect, your number one source for all things stationery. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of office supplies, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Ahmed Atef, pencil perfect has come a long way from its beginnings in Shibin Elkom. When Ahmed Atef first started out, their passion for eco-friendly office supplies drove them to start their own business.

We now serve customers all over Egypt and are thrilled to be a part of the stationery industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.

FAQ

What types of stationery products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of stationery products, including notebooks, pens, pencils, planners, office supplies, art supplies, and much more. Browse our categories to find the perfect items for your needs.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website and viewing your order history.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Please visit our Return Policy page for detailed instructions on how to process a return.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary based on the destination. Please check our Shipping Information page for more details.

Can I customize my stationery order?

Yes, we offer customization options for many of our products, such as personalized notebooks and custom-printed pens. Visit our Custom Orders page to learn more about the available options and how to place a custom order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options. You can select your preferred payment method during the checkout process.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team by emailing support@stationeryshop.com or by calling our toll-free number at 1-800-123-4567. Our support team is available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 5 PM EST.

Do you offer bulk discounts for large orders?

Yes, we offer bulk discounts for large orders. Please contact our sales team at sales@stationeryshop.com with your requirements, and we will provide you with a customized quote.
